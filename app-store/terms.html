Glow Handle Terms of Use

Effective date: July 4, 2026

Purpose

Glow Handle creates local visual IDs, idp1 contact cards, protected messages, and sealed notes/files. It is not a crypto wallet, exchange, financial service, mining tool, or blockchain client. It does not custody assets, trade assets, connect to chains, or provide investment functionality.

Random seed responsibility

Your random seed is the key to your ID. If it is deleted or lost, the developer cannot recover it from a server. If another person obtains it, that person may control the corresponding ID and open content sealed for that account.

Pro purchase

Glow Handle Pro is a one-time in-app purchase handled by Apple through the App Store. The final price, tax, purchase confirmation, restore flow, and refunds are handled by Apple. Pro unlocks bulk contact import/export, batch candidate generation, advanced card styles, the Pro app icon, encrypted notebook features, and file/audio protected messages.

Randomized results

Batch candidates and rarity styles are randomized tools. Buying Pro removes certain daily limits but does not guarantee any specific rarity tier. Approximate rarity odds are: Common 55.0%, Uncommon 25.0%, Rare 12.0%, Epic 5.8%, Legendary 2.0%, Mythic 0.2%.

Security boundaries

Encryption features protect local content and content you intentionally share. They do not protect against a compromised device, malicious keyboards, malicious screenshots, clipboard leakage, or content you intentionally forward.
