Common questions

Do I need an account?
No. The app works locally and does not require sign-in.

Can the developer recover my random seed?
No. There is no server-side recovery. Back up your random seed somewhere you trust.

Does the app upload contacts, messages, notes, or files?
No. Contact cards, protected messages, notes, and selected files are processed locally on device.

How do I restore Pro?
Open Settings, choose Glow Handle Pro, then tap Restore Purchases.
